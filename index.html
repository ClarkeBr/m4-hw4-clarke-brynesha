Mahalia Burkmar, known professionally as Mahalia, is a British singer, songwriter, and actress, recognized for her soulful voice and blend of R&B, neo-soul, and reggae influences. Born on May 1, 1998, in Leicester, England, Mahalia started singing and writing songs at a young age, signing her first record deal with Asylum Records when she was just 13.
